❓ Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our products, services, and policies.

How accurate is the Laser Eye Scanner?
Our EyeScan Pro achieves 99.97% biometric identification accuracy using a multi-spectrum infrared and UV laser array. It captures over 1,200 unique data points per scan in under 0.3 seconds.
Is my biometric data stored securely?
All biometric data is encrypted with AES-256 before transmission and stored in our HIPAA and GDPR-compliant data centers. We never sell personal data to third parties.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards via Stripe, PayPal, wire transfers for enterprise orders, and cryptocurrency payments (BTC, ETH) upon request.
Do you offer a money-back guarantee?
Yes! All hardware products come with a 30-day full money-back guarantee. Software subscriptions include a 14-day free trial.
How do promo codes work?
Enter your promo code at checkout. Try NEXGEN20 (20% off), WELCOME50 ($50 off first order), or TECH15 (15% off). Codes are case-insensitive and may have expiry dates.
How do I delete my account?
Navigate to Account → Security & Privacy → Delete Account. The process is fully self-service with identity verification. All data is permanently removed within 30 days per GDPR Article 17.
How do I contact support?
Reach us at moghalukaito@gmail.com or call 346 604 7133. Our support team is available Mon–Fri 9AM–8PM EST, weekends 10AM–5PM EST.
